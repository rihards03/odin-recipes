Sausage Balls

These 3-ingredient sausage balls are a real crowd pleaser, whether you serve them as a breakfast or brunch treat, a party appetizer, a game-day nibble, or a holiday hors d'oeuvre.

Savory sausage, store-bought biscuit mix, and sharp Cheddar cheese team up for savory, cheesy flavor that can't be beat. These are so yummy! My family makes every Christmas morning

  • Savory sausage
  • Store-bought biscuit mix
  • Sharp Cheddar cheese
  • Savory
  1. Mix the sausage and baking mix together.
  2. Stir in the cheese.
  3. Roll the mixture into balls. Transfer to a baking sheet.
  4. Bake until the balls are golden brown and the sausage is cooked through.
